Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. Your trust is important to us, and we are dedicated to safeguarding your information.

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and store your personal data when you use our website, products, or services. By accessing or using our website, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

We may collect the following personal data from you, depending on the nature of your interaction with us:

  • Identity Information:

  • Contact Information:
    Address, email address, phone number.
  • Transaction Details:
    Purchase history, payment methods, billing, and shipping addresses.
  • Demographic Information:
    Preferences, interests, and other relevant information for customer experience enhancement.

Your personal data will be processed for the following purposes:

  • To fulfill legal and regulatory obligations

Disclosure of Personal Data

Your personal data may be shared with:

Data Retention

We will retain your personal data as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes stated above, or as required by law. Upon the completion of such purposes, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.
